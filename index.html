Story So Far

Celestial are being who serve the Almighty while protecting mortals and defeating monsters. You are the Celestial apprentice of Aqualia, the Guardian of Angel Falls, training to take his place. But due to unknown circumstances the Observatory, home to the Celestials is disturbed and you find yourself in the mortal Realm, wingless and without a halo.


System

Dragon Quest 9: Sentinels of the Starry Skies is currently a Nintendo DS Exclusive with a potential phone port in the making.


Gameplay

Create and customize your unique party members! Unlock and play 10 different classes! Hundreds of weapons and armour that affect your overworld character! 3D Graphics! Explore Grottoes! 200 Quests! And a 30+ Hour Campaign!
